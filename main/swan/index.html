The Origami Swan

This simple and elegant origami swan is easy to make, but very impressive.
You can learn how to make it below!

Step One: Getting Started

To make a swan, you will need to start with a square piece of paper

Step Two: Preliminary Folds

Next, you must fold the paper in half diagonally, and fold two of the raw
edges to that crease

Step Three: Narrowing the Model

Next, using the guides shown in the picture, fold each side into thirds,
so you are left with a skinny arrow shape.

Step Four: Folding into a Swan

Using the guidelines in these images, fold the model so it resembles a flatter swan.

Step Five: Finishing Touches

Fold the bottom part of the crane like a mountain and it should fold the rest
of the swan to follow

There you have it! You have learned how to make an origami swan.
